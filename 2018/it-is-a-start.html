It is a start

Published: Thu 15 March 2018
By mrmn-dev

In Postcards.

Hello and welcome to my github hosted static website. There isn't much going on right now and since I have a family and a full time job I am not expecting this ever to be non stop action. Hopefully every now and then I do get back here.

I do have some general goals. I am not exactly sure if github can help me with those goals.

One thing I would like to do is get organized. The value of organization isn't as much as it used to be. Google can find everything for you. However I have not yet been able to get google to find anything I have forgotten. Perhaps this could be a place where I put things as a reminder. Like a note that you take to the grocery store that says "bread, milk, eggs"...the same note week after week.

The other thing I would like to do is learn. It would be good to have a little direction in that learning. Organization would help with direction. Look! I am learning a little about github pages and Pelican!
